* **The Momentum Factor**: Drives the slight tech overweight, locking onto top-tier generative AI hyperscalers.

### 2. Vanguard's Mutualized Passive Stewardship
Vanguard's framework is fundamentally structural, optimizing purely for **tracking error minimization** and transactional cost reduction. 
* Because Vanguard is owned by its funds (and thus, its investors), its portfolios are built as pure conduits of market return, holding thousands of micro-cap equities that institutional active managers avoid.
* This explains the sprawling **8,114 position count**, securing every fraction of market capitalization to preserve strict index fidelity.
